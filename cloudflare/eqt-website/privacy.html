Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

At EQT (Easy QR Transfer), accessible from eqt.net.im, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by EQT and how we use it.

Local Network Operation (Zero Cloud Storage)

EQT is designed as a peer-to-peer (P2P) local network file transfer utility. All file transfers and communications between your devices occur strictly within your local area network (LAN). Your files, texts, and messages are transferred directly from one device to another and are never uploaded to any external servers, databases, or cloud environments. We do not collect, read, or store any files or chat logs transferred via EQT.

Licensing & Device Fingerprinting

For activation of Plus licenses, EQT communicates with our serverless DRM API located at Cloudflare to verify license validity. This check transmits a non-reversible cryptographic hash of your hardware fingerprint (constructed from motherboard UUID, CPU serial, and disk serial) to ensure license limits are respected. This data does not contain any personally identifiable information (PII), is completely anonymized, and is stored securely in our database.

Third-Party Payment Reseller (Paddle)

We use Paddle.com as our online reseller and Merchant of Record (MoR) for all Plus licenses and subscription payments. When you purchase EQT Plus, your payment checkout is handled directly by Paddle. Paddle collects personal and financial transaction data, including your name, email, billing address, and payment credentials. Paddle's checkout process and data handling are governed by Paddle's own Privacy Policy, which you can review on their official site.

Paddle shares basic non-sensitive order details (such as your email address, purchase date, order status, and license type) with EQT so we can issue your license code and provide customer support. We do not store or have access to your credit card details or bank credentials.

Cookies

This website uses cookies to store visitor language preferences. We do not use tracking, marketing, or advertising cookies.

Contact us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@eqt.net.im.
